Interpolation Schminterpolation - CTK 203

This project was a part of an assignment for CTK 203. The main criteria for the assignment was that it had to be an interpolation of something, well I had a slightly outside of the box idea (still acually interpolated something though). When I was looking up the definition of interpolation I also found the mathematical definition of interpolation and I decided to base my project around using panning to match the lines in the picture (a quadratic interpolation).

Final Composition - CTK 203

This was a group composition project from CTK 203, our group's idea was a monster destroying a city, the whole main soundscape of the second part was made by one person in the group (big props Wyatt), and dialougue was then recorded in class. After recording the group was split into two smaller ones, where I was the main editor of the track, while the others found resources and provided ideas and feedback.

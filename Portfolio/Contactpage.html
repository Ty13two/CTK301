About Me

I am currently a sophmore at ISU majoring in CTK in the Audio and Music Production sequence. I also play the trumpet in the ISU Pep Band. I enjoy listening to music, playing video games, and hanging out with my cat.
This is my cat, Monkey.

Look at how handsome he is!

My interest in audio and music production really started with theater in High School, where I found myself in charge of our sound console. I think producing music is extremely cool, and I've been playing music for awhile, but that's not what I envision myself doing in the future. I'm not really sure what I'd rather find myself doing, but it's probably closer to how I started, with a sound console bringing together different sounds to make one cohesive soundscape or story.
